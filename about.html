About Us

Brothers Mario and Luigi have been providing reliable plumbing services to Bullhead City and the surrounding area for over 20 years. Our small family business was built on a foundation of hard work, honesty and a genuine love for helping our neighbors. We treat every project—whether big or small—with the same care and attention to detail.

Our Story

What started as two brothers and a pickup truck has grown into Bullhead City’s most trusted plumbing team. Over the years we’ve invested in the latest equipment and training, but our commitment to customer satisfaction has never changed. When you call Mario & Luigi Plumbing, you’re treated like family.
